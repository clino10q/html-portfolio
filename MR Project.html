Top 10 Best Anime To Watch According To Ayomide

These are my personal best anime to watch

10. HunterxHunter

Solid anime to watch. It involves two boys on a journey together. I'll rate a 8.8/10

9. Darling in the Franxx

It's one of the only two mecha animes that i like. It contains romance and drama also. I would recommend this. It gets a 9.0/10

8. Wind Breaker

It's one of my favourite animes ever due to the fact that it contains fist fighting(something i love seeing when watching an anime). It's a 9.1/10 anime for sure

7. Fire Force

It's a lovely anime to watch. It has one my favourite side characters ever and also a little bit of fan service. It's a solid 9.3/10 anime for me

6. Blue Lock

It was my first sports anime and one of two to make this list. I'm a football lover and seeing football made to look much more fascinating is just cool. It gets a 9.4/10 rating from me

5. Jujutsu Kaisen

It is one of the best new gen anime and trust me, it is awesome!!!. It is also a great anime for starters. It's a 9.4/10 anime

4. My Hero Academia

At first i didnt want to watch this but when i actually decide to, it became the anime with over 100 episodes that i finished in the shortest time. It has great and lovely characters. It's a solid 9.5/10

3. Kuroko's Basketball

Now we're getting into my top 3 anime. This anime is my favourite sports anime ever. The characters are all unique, incredible storyline, great matches and a lot more. It is a 9.8 anime for me

2. Demon Slayer

What is there not to love about this anime? Has incredible characters, a great storyline, the best animation i've ever seen, incredible fight scenes. I could go on and on about this anime. You've got to give it a watch if you haven't yet. It is a solid 9.9/10 anime

1. Naruto

This anime right here is the best anime anyone could ever watch in their lifetime. It's protagonist is my favourite character(Naruto), has one the best villians of all time, is in the legendary anime group, The BIG Three, has amazing character development, amazing fights. It's just awesome altogether. It would recommend to anime starters. It is a great first anime(It was mine). It is a 10/10 anime
